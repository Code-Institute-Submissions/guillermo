Guillermo
Brachetta

Musician

I wish I could carve my own chess figures.
I wish I could write the novel of the century.
I wish I could build my own house.
I wish I could paint the Amsterdam sunset in all its spring colours.
I can make music, which is the miracle of having all that together.

I love being a musician.
I could have my dream day playing basso continuo and chamber music with friends and colleagues.
I have a strong connection with the music of Wilhelm Friedemann Bach and wished it was better understood.
I cannot hold a conversation or read a book if there’s good music playing (but I can cry).
I find being onstage irresistible. Thanks to the music I travel and meet fascinating people.
I’m extremely fortunate.

Please visit to discover my recordings, get to know more about my music, browse images and video and find out information about my upcoming events.
